**getEmployee**
----

  * **Version History:**

	TASS v54.0 - Method Added

* **Version:**

  3

* **Permission:**

   Employee/HR > Employees > View

* **Method:**

  `GET | POST`
  
*  **Params:**

   **Required:**
 
   `userid [string]`

   **Optional:**
 
   none

   **Conditional:**
